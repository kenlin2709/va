About Vape Lab Group

Australian-crafted vape juice and a curated lineup of premium tobacco — smooth flavour, reliable performance, and quality you can taste.

Our Story

Vape Lab Group was founded by a small team of flavour obsessives in Australia who wanted to bring a more thoughtful approach to the vape and tobacco aisle. We started in a lab — literally — testing PG/VG ratios until each bottle delivered the kind of smooth, satisfying draw we'd want for ourselves.

Today, we ship Australia-wide with a growing catalogue that includes our signature lab-crafted e-liquids and a hand-picked selection of iconic global tobacco brands.
